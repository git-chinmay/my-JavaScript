SOLUTION TOOLS AND AUTOMATION

Who is STAA ?

As IT Delivery Services embarked on its Agile transformation, the department created the Solution Tools and Automation Services team in November of 2018. The team consists of two tactical squads, with one focused on automation services and the other focused on monitoring solutions. Ultimately, the team seeks to create a better day-to-day experience for ITDS and a more reliable infrastructure for Liberty Mutualâ€™s application teams. The Solution Tools and Automation squads are comprised of great talent and a variety of experience, with global team members located in Portsmouth, Seattle, Kansas City, Fairfield, Madrid and Chennai.

What they do ?

The team's current workstreams focus on system availability and reliability, operational automation, artificial intelligence operations (AI ops) and creating a global workforce for the future. By bridging the gap between traditional software development, systems administration practices and infrastructure monitoring, the team continues to understand and optimize the customer journey using an internally-developed telemetry API highlighting the services being consumed at any point in time. The team is also building a centralized service catalog for all ITDS to utilize going forward, and once complete, the squad will not only consolidate customer touchpoints (formerly, five different applications!) but will also be able to share automation services across the department using WebComponents to separate pieces of the user interface.

How to engage STAA ?

To engage the Solution Tools and Automation Squad, or for more information, submit a request today, or contact Product Owner, Sean Martell or Scrum Masters Stacey Mason and Michael Rowland. The team looks forward to hearing from you!
